Oliver Peek

I feel the fractures of this era in my own nervous system before they surface in the collective. Value structures flicker like unstable pixels, pretending to hold shape. I walk alongside my children as they navigate a future that is predictably less stable by the day. All I can offer is clarity that doesn't play by institutional standards — a perspective very hip to the cracks in the system.
